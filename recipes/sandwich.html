Sandwich Recipe

This is how to make one of the many sandwiches I ate over the course of my legendary high school career.

  • bread
  • mayonnaise
  • ham
  • cheese
  1. Place the bread flat and lather the insides with mayo.
  2. Place the piece of cheese on either piece of bread.
  3. Put the slices of ham in the middle.
  4. Put your new tasty sandwich together!!!
